Is this an overdose? Signs include:

  • The person will not wake up or respond to your voice or touch
  • Their breathing is very slow, irregular or has stopped
  • The center part of their eye is very small — sometimes called “pinpoint pupils”
  • Their fingernails and lips turning blue or purple
  • Slow heartbeat and/or low blood pressure
